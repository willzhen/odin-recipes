Nước chấm

Ingredients

  • 10 birds eye chilis
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1.5 Tbsp white sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Lime Juice
  • 2.5 Tbsp Fish Sauce
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. In a mortar and pestle, pulverize garlic until crushed
  2. Add chilis and continue pulverizing until it becomes a paste
  3. Add white sugar and mix in lime juice, fish sauce and hot water until fully incorporated
